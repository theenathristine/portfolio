About Me

With over 9 years of experience as a registered nurse, I now focus on supporting healthcare professionals as a Virtual Assistant. My background includes managing patient care plans, fostering clear communication with physicians, and ensuring accurate medical record organization. These skills translate seamlessly into handling administrative tasks like appointment scheduling, email management, and calendar coordination.

Dedicated to maintaining data privacy and confidentiality, I am currently enhancing my expertise in HIPAA compliance and EHR systems. My mission is to empower healthcare providers by relieving their administrative burdens, enabling them to focus on patient care. Known for my reliability and adaptability, I strive to deliver organized, secure, and empathetic support tailored to the unique needs of the healthcare field.
